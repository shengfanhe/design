Project Info

Privacy and user data protection is one of the most critical business domains for TikTok global operation. As the regulations from the operating regions become more and more strict, privacy is also a top challenge for the company. Over the 2 years, I worked with the privacy engineering team and global legal team to build digital products to solve the privacy compliance issues at the corporation level.

Privacy control to be orchestrated.

Primarily used by legal team and developers, the SaaS web APP plays an important role in bridging the gaps between the privacy laws and complicated technical world.
