Project Info

This product was originally developed for the compliance requirements according to the EU Article 17 and US Digital Millennium Copyright Act aka DMCA. After the techonology was built, we realized the technical capability of matching content between multimedia assets and TikTok posts can be used for more scenarios given the facts that so many people are creating and viewing videos on this social network.

Video fingerprinting in a nutshell

Right holders upload their copyright content to MediaMatch as assets.

The video fingerprinting technology will scan the videos on TikTok and identify those are using the clips from right holders’ assets.

Copyright matching provides rightholders the authority to allow or block the usage of their content on TikTok, which build the trust for more partnership and opportunities.

By matching between rightholders' assets and TikTok videos, we are able to generate data analysis of the assets through user's interaction with those matched content from the social media network.
