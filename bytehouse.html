Project Info

Bytedance has been using ClickHouse as their datawarehouse over the decade. As the business grow, the data engineering team is continuously making improvements based on this open source data technology. The performance, scalability and stability make it a valuable B2B product as a SaaS business.

Bytedance's high performance data warehouse has been the analytical engine for the APP factory. The advantages make it competitive as a SaaS product.

Speed up Time to Insight

Support real-time analysis based on Streaming Data Loading (Apache Kafka, AWS Kinesis) with a unified platform. Accelerate the process of time to insight.

High Performance

Achieve high performance by Vectorized Query Execution and Column-oriented Storage.

Read-Write Separation

Achieve scalable computing and distributed storage by the separate Storage & Compute Layer.

Low Maintenance

A self-serve platform with lower maintenance, fully managing your data with ByteHouse.
