Project Info

This is a web portal for MCN agencies who manage creators on TikTok. We used to collaborate with agencies through offline channels, but that requires too much labour for our operation team. We built this CRM tool for agencies so that the request of data can be more self-service. The tooling streamlined the agency workflow and made it scalable, which also saved a lot of inhouse operation team's manpower.

MCN agencies are the incubators of TikTok creators. They are important partners in TikTok ecosystem to help grow the creators and content.

MCNs drive growth and earn profit, it's a win-win.

This web APP provides MCN agencies a creator relationship management tool for them track the performance of creator's posts and manage the lifecycle of their collaboration.
